CTIA (Certified Threat Intelligence Analyst) – Introduction

CTIA (Certified Threat Intelligence Analyst) is a globally recognized certification from EC-Council that focuses on Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI). It teaches security professionals how to collect, analyze, and use threat intelligence to identify and mitigate cyber threats before they impact an organization.

Exam Details

Questions: 50
